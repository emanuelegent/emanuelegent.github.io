About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the NOAA Geophysical Fluid Dynamics Laboratory and Princeton University, working on boundary-layer turbulence, air-sea interactions, and extreme midlatitude cyclones. My current research explores how turbulent momentum fluxes modulate wind and precipitation extremes in a warming climate using high-resolution Earth system models.

Research Interests

  • Boundary-layer turbulence and sub-grid scale modeling
  • Air-sea interaction in extreme weather events
  • Machine learning for climate model parameterization
  • High-resolution modeling of midlatitude cyclones
